Fantasy Insider’s Weekend Picks: Draftstars 19 – 22 August
Our top Draftstars picks and team of the week for AFL and NRL this weekend.

Happy Friday and welcome to the Draftstars edition of the Fantasy Insider Weekend Picks. We’re in the nal stretch now, and 6 weeks from now we’ll be talking about the Grand Finals, with just two teams to choose from in each sport. This week we have a full slate though, so let’s jump to the games.

AFL GRAND FINAL TIPS

In the AFL there’s a number of games this week with low totals predicted. In fact so many that we’ll take the opposite approach to normal, and tell you to look to the Brisbane/Geelong game, the Adelaide derby, as well as GWS/Fremantle and Collingwood/Gold Coast for your higher scoring contests. GWS and Geelong are both favoured by 60-70 points, with the Bulldogs 48.5 favorites, and Adelaide at 35.5 also worth a note.

HOT 30

In the NRL, games got underway last night with the Broncos continuing their return to form vs Canterbury. The rest of the weekend features a lot of games totaled between 40 and 44, with Newcastle over Gold Coast, North QLD over the Warriors, and Canberra over Parramatta those favoured by over 2 tries.
